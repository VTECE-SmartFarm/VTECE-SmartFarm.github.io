This is VTECE - SmartFarm Research Group!

Who We Are

We are the SmartFarm Research Group, a team dedicated to integrating machine learning with agricultural practices. Our group consists of researchers and technologists passionate about advancing farming through innovative.

What We Do?

Our work focuses on leveraging machine learning to tackle farming-related challenges, particularly in animal analysis. We study and develop cutting-edge techniques to modernize agriculture, aiming to enhance productivity, sustainability, and efficiency. By applying these advanced technologies, we strive to transform theoretical research into practical solutions that can be implemented in real-world farming scenarios.

How to get involved?

There are several ways to get involved with our research group. Whether you are a student looking for internship opportunities, a researcher interested in collaborative projects, or a farmer seeking to modernize your practices, we welcome your interest and participation. Contact us to learn more about how you can contribute to and benefit from our work in smart farming innovations.
